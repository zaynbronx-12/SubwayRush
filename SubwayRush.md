# Subway Rush

An endless runner game inspired by **Subway Surfers**, built in **Unity**.

---

## 🎮 Features
✅ Lane switching (3 lanes)  
✅ Coins & scoring  
✅ Trains & obstacles  
✅ Android-ready  
✅ Free assets & sounds
